About Mochii-Mochii

The founder of Mochii-Mochii Nathalie Willemse has traveled Asia and wanted to bring back something from her travels. So she started this mochi project to share all that she has learned from the history of mochi. We, as her team, are very honored to be able to dive into this culture and experience new things. We also want to give you the opportunity to explore it.
